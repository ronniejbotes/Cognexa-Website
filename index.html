Questions, answered

The things businesses actually ask us before they build.

How much does an AI receptionist or chatbot cost in South Africa?

Every build is scoped to your business, so pricing depends on the channels you need (web, WhatsApp, voice), your call and chat volumes, and the systems we integrate with. Scoping is free, you get a fixed monthly quote in Rand before committing, and there’s no minimum contract. For the full breakdown of what drives the numbers, read our guide to AI receptionist costs in South Africa.

Is a WhatsApp AI chatbot POPIA compliant?

Yes — when it’s built correctly. We design around POPIA’s consent and minimality principles: customers opt in before marketing messages, every conversation offers an opt-out, data is kept only as long as it’s needed, and processing agreements cover the tools involved. Our POPIA compliance guide covers the details.

Do I need the WhatsApp Business API to run a chatbot?

For real automation at business scale, yes. The free WhatsApp Business app doesn’t allow bots — automated messaging runs on the WhatsApp Business Platform (API), which we set up and manage as part of every chatbot build, including verified sender status and approved message templates.

Will an AI voice agent sound robotic — and when does a human take over?

Modern voice models handle everyday calls naturally, and we tune pacing, accent and vocabulary to your brand before launch. Just as important: the agent knows its limits. When a call needs judgement or empathy, it hands over warmly to your team with the full conversation context — mid-call if needed. For the honest three-way comparison, see AI voice agent vs IVR vs human receptionist.
